About me

Nikta Javanfar

Product Manager
Currently residing in Ottawa

I am a wanna-be-entrepreneur who loves building products that help people and businesses. I consider product management the most exhilirating role within an orginization: I get to work with customers, partners, decision-makers, engineers, salespeople, marketers, and all the roles in between! My aim is to bring clarity and sanity to any project I work on, and to align all team members on the product vision.

My education in mathematics and physics drives my analytical and data-based approach to product development. My upbringing in the arts, with a focus in classical music and violin, has taught me to appreciate beauty, and bring creativity to my work.

I am always looking to collaborate with others on exciting projects. I hope that this website allows you to get to know me a little better. I'd love to get to know you better too, so make sure you leave me a note to say hi!
